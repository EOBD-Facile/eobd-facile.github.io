FAQ

Which vehicles are supported?

Most OBD2-compliant cars from 2001 onward, with enhanced manufacturer codes for European and select American brands.

What interface do I need?

Any compatible ELM327 adapter (USB or Bluetooth). Ensure it's OBD2 certified.

Does it work on Apple Silicon Macs?

Yes, fully optimized for M-series chips and macOS Sonoma/Ventura.

Is setup complicated?

No—plug in, launch the app, and scan. No subscriptions or extra software needed.
